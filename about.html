About the Researcher

Jeran Mark — focused on accessible AI

Jeran Mark is a final-year BSc (Hons) in Information Technology student at SLIIT, with a focus on applied machine learning for social impact. For SignSight, he designed the full recognition stack — data acquisition protocols, landmark-based feature engineering, Bi-LSTM / Bi-GRU modelling, evaluation, and the live inference client.

He is interested in making computer vision cheaper, clearer, and more useful outside the lab, especially for communities underserved by mainstream language technology. This research project is his contribution toward that goal.
