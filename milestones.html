Project Milestones

The SignSight research project is assessed across five milestones covering proposal, progress reviews, a final assessment, and a viva. Mark allocations reflect the SLIIT IT4010 Research Project guidelines.

Project Proposal

Topic assessment, proposal report submission, and proposal presentation covering the research problem, literature survey, methodology, and feasibility.

July 2025 12% Marks Completed

Progress Presentation 1

50% project progress review — dataset collection, landmark extraction pipeline, baseline Bi-LSTM training, and preliminary evaluation results.

October 2025 15% Marks Scheduled

Progress Presentation 2

90% progress — full Bi-LSTM and Bi-GRU evaluation, integration with the SignSight web client, and user feedback from pilot testing.

February 2026 18% Marks Upcoming

Final Assessment

Submission of the final individual research report, camera-ready IEEE paper, research poster, and the final project presentation to the evaluation panel.

May 2026 20% Marks Upcoming

Viva & Demonstration

Final viva voce, live demonstration of the recognition system, commercialization video, and user testing session with a research team member.

June 2026 10% Marks Upcoming
