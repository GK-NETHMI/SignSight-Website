How to add your files

Every View / Download link above points at a file in the assets/ folder. You have two easy options:

  1. Local files — drop PDFs into project-website/assets/ with the filenames shown in README.md.
  2. Google Drive / OneDrive links — upload your files to the cloud, get a "Anyone with link can view" share URL, then open documents.html and replace assets/filename.pdf with that URL in both the View and Download links for that document.
